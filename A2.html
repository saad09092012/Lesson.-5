Facts about cat

Cats can make about 100 different sounds! 🐱 This is significantly more than dogs, which can only produce around 10. Cats use these sounds—like meows, purrs, hisses, and growls—to communicate with humans and other animals. Interestingly, adult cats meow almost exclusively to humans, not to other cats!

Cats have a third eyelid! 🐾 It's called the nictitating membrane, and it’s a thin, pale layer that moves diagonally across the eye. This extra eyelid helps protect and moisten their eyes without obstructing their vision. You might catch a glimpse of it when your cat is very relaxed or sleepy.
